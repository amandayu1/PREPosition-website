Our story

Who We Are

PREPosition is a student-run initiative that is dedicated to helping communities grow, while allowing youth to be a part of it. As a writing organization, we hope to inspire teens to develop innovative solutions for the future. This is done through a series of webinars and contests, where panels of highly-qualified professionals and university students speak about their experiences. They also judge and provide feedback for their younger peers during these events. Our objective is to raise money and increase transparency in the perspectives of different persons. Initiated by a group of student leaders, we ensure that youth are given the chances to express their ideas, while also losing ignorance. Change is coming; and we are PREPared for it.


This Year's Theme

The coronavirus has made a negative influence in our communities. They have affected businesses; schools; and our own lives. This year’s theme will be regarding the future of COVID-19 -- and what we should do to help mitigate any further harm.
