What is the future after the coronavirus?

This year, our theme will be on the following: what is the future after the coronavirus? (How do we rise again, and what solutions can we implement to help us get there?)

Participants are invited to write a 250-750 word essay on this subject. The pieces will be judged by a panel of credible teachers, professors, university students, business owners, government officials -- and many more. The articles will be evaluated through creativity; language articulation; etc.

There will be an entry fee for this competition, and the money will be directly paid through GoFundMe for transparency purposes. If students would like feedback, there is also an additional cost. All proceeds made will be donated to the winner’s school, where it will be spent on purchasing sanitation products for schools. One dominant concern is the safety of students and teachers, as we begin to return to education. Thus, PREPosition is dedicated to help fund these communities and allow them to have increased protection. (note: if the school does not accept donations, the winner may select any COVID-19 related charity.)
