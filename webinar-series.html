Another element that will be included are webinars, in which individuals who have been impacted by the coronavirus will speak about their own experiences. First, we are inviting a front-line worker who will discuss his/her/their time in their job. Second, a teacher will speak, regarding the changes made to continue teaching. Third, a business owner who has struggled will be interviewed. Last, a new business owner who has begun a project in the midst of quarantine will be asked to spread some positivity and motivation. These webinars will increase the amount of awareness participants have for people in these roles. They will look at different perspectives and lose their ignorance.

These webinars will be free of charge and open to anyone. However, it is recommended that participants donate some sanitation products to their schools.
